# Theft in Baltimore: Contributing Factors and Potential Solutions 
If you live in Baltimore, you have a 1 in 15 chance of being a victim of any crime. It is arguably America’s most dangerous city with burglary, larceny and robbery representing 72% of its total crime. These three categories, all falling under the umbrella of “theft”, are addressed in this report; providing findings on their contributing factors and potential solutions.
